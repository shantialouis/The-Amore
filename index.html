About Us

The Amore Gardens is a world-class exclusive and urban lifestyle outdoor solution expressed in garden style for all season and all-purpose from intimate private live or love affairs to corporate events and social gatherings, with state-of-the-art deliberate features put together with accent of pizzazz safety and class. Located in the prime of Lekki, Lagos, The Amore Gardens allows for easy traction, attraction, and access. The amore can host extremely private events in single digits to large events holding over 2000 people.
